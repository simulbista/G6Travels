Europe

With its layers of history and overlapping of cultures, Europe is a treasure trove of everything
from the medieval and ancient to the baroque and art nouveau. Couple this with Arctic conditions
in the north and beautiful Mediterranean climates in the south, and there’s a whole side of nature
to go with the main dish of culture that can be found in this wonderful continent.
Sinking cities, leaning towers, and mystic old towns are mixed with Catholic churches, deep lochs,
and sweeping fjords. This hotbed of history and civilization makes for an undeniably exciting place
for any traveler to explore
